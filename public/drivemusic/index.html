About

Drive Music Recording Studio, Swindon. Established 1986. Over the years, Drive studio has worked with many well known Singer/songwriters and established Bands, including Swindons own Rock giants XTC. Recording, Mixing, Mastering and Production services available. Tape Archiving - From your old tapes to Digital format.

Studio

Recording on :- Digital - Logic Pro 10. Outboard equipment :- Lexicon, Drawmer, Focusright, DBX. Air Conditioned :- Live Room, Control Room, Vocal Booth.

Pricing

The studio comprises a live room, relaxation/band room as well as a dedicated vocal booth. The studio is Air Conditioned with video room to room monitoring. Drive studios creates a relaxed and productive environment. Free tea and coffee is available.
